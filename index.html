Reliability, Innovation and Quality.
RELIABILITY

Recognised as one of the most trusted global partners in the watch-strap industry, we are humbled to have come this far in our journey of delivering consistent results of top notch quality backed by constant innovation. We ensure that our internal process is constantly monitored and that quality checks are met at every level, so that the final product reflects the precision. Our adherence to our core values, regardless of the changing times, has helped us emerge and sustain as the most reliable partner.

INNOVATION

We have established ourselves as a pioneer of innovation, in the watch-strap industry with dedicated efforts to ensure novelty and beauty reflecting in each of our watch strap. At VBL Innovations, our constant efforts are to bring forth freshness in ideas, that are fashionable and sustainable, with the help of cutting-edge technology.

QUALITY

Our foremost priority has always been to maintain top-notch quality. We leave no stone unturned to ensure that our quality checks are executed seamlessly at every stage of the manufacturing process. From materials’ inspection and strong process controls to checks during various stages of the manufacturing, we inspect the tiniest of the details to ensure our straps are of the finest quality.

About Us

VBL Innovations Pvt. Ltd. was established in the year 1995. From our inception till date, we have stood by our unwavering commitment to delivering the highest quality watch straps, with exceptionally high standards of Reliability, Innovation and Quality.

Every strap that we make has in it an uncanny resemblance to nature and along with an inherent belonging to the modern world. While we abide by the industry’s trends, we have found the secret formula to remain relevant yet timeless in our designs and execution.
